What Convex provides
A database to store your data.
Backend functions (queries, mutations, actions) that your frontend calls.
Real-time updates so your UI updates automatically when data changes.
Authentication integration with providers like Clerk or Auth.js.
Hosting for your backend, so you don't have to manage servers.
